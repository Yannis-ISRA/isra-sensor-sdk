# Introduction

Welcome to the ISRA Sensor SDK documentation.

Our project focuses on designing and manufacturing passive UHF temperature/humidity sensors for demanding industrial environments.

## Why this approach is different

- **100% passive**: no cell, no battery
- **Zero energy maintenance**: no battery replacement planning
- **Zero sensor wiring**: no cable to pull to each measuring point

## Operational benefits

- Faster commissioning, especially in retrofit projects
- Fewer recurring maintenance interventions
- Fewer failures linked to local sensor power supply

## Environmental benefits

- No battery to produce, transport, and recycle
- Less waste over the full lifecycle
- Compact format (smaller than a credit card) and very low material usage

## Economic benefits (TCO)

- Lower total cost of ownership (installation + maintenance + operation)
- Scalable deployment without wiring complexity growth
- Better field reliability thanks to simpler architecture
